My Musical Interests


I have loved music since I was very young, and I have composed music for as long as I can remember. I like experimenting with layered vocals, various production styles, unique synthesizer sounds, and intricate melodies that are unpredictable. Furthermore, I like painting a picture with the sounds I choose to use. I always think of imagery behind all my music, and I try to channel that imagery or art in the way I utilize instruments and construct melodies and harmonies.

Some of my musical inspirations:

  • FKA twigs
  • Amy Winehouse
  • Janelle Monae
  • Fiona Apple
  • Minnie Riperton
  • Yves Tumor
  • Kalandra
